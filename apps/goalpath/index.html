Features

Built for people who actually want to finish

Not another to-do app. A complete system that connects your daily actions to the goals they serve.

Daily Focus

Open GoalPath each morning and see your priorities for the day. No scrolling through lists. No guessing what matters. Just today's actions, tied directly to your goals.

Break Down Goals

Split any goal into phases, then into tasks, then into daily to-dos. Suddenly, "learn Spanish" becomes "practice vocabulary for 15 minutes." The path forward is always clear.

Track Progress

Watch your progress rings fill up as you complete tasks. Daily streaks, completion stats, and visual milestones show you exactly how far you've come.

Organized System

Four layers of organization (goals, phases, tasks, to-dos) keep everything connected. You always know how today's work fits into the bigger picture.
