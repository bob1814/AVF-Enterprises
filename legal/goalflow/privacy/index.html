GoalFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/10/2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the GoalFlow mobile application, operated by AVF Enterprises LLC, and does not apply to other applications operated by the company.

GoalFlow respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the GoalFlow app.

1. Information We Collect

GoalFlow does not require you to create an account.

Information you provide

  • Goals, phases, tasks, to-dos, and journal entries you create in the app
  • App preferences and settings

This information is stored locally on your device.

Feedback you choose to submit

If you choose to submit feedback through the app, any information you include (such as written feedback or an optional email address) is provided voluntarily and used only to respond to your message or improve the app. Feedback is submitted using your device's email app.

Automatically collected information

GoalFlow may collect limited, anonymous app usage and performance data to improve app stability and user experience. This data does not identify you personally.

GoalFlow does not collect:

  • Names
  • Required email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Advertising identifiers

2. Data Storage

  • Your data is stored locally on your device.
  • If iCloud is enabled, your data may be backed up using Apple's iCloud services.
  • GoalFlow does not operate its own servers for storing user data.

3. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.

GoalFlow does not have access to:

  • Your payment information
  • Your Apple ID
  • Your billing details

4. Notifications

GoalFlow uses local notifications to remind you about to-dos, tasks, phases, or goals.

  • Notifications are optional
  • You can enable or disable them at any time
  • Notification data never leaves your device

5. Third-Party Services

GoalFlow uses Apple-provided services such as:

  • App Store
  • StoreKit
  • iCloud
  • WidgetKit

GoalFlow does not sell or share user data with third parties.

6. Data Deletion

  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Any data stored in iCloud can be managed or deleted through your Apple ID iCloud settings.

7. Children's Privacy

GoalFlow is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent changes.
