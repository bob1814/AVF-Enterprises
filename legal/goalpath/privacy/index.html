GoalPath Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the GoalPath mobile application, operated by AVF Enterprises LLC, and does not apply to other applications operated by the company.

GoalPath respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the GoalPath app.

1. Information We Collect

GoalPath does not require you to create an account.

Information you provide

  • Goals, phases, tasks, to-dos, and journal entries you create in the app
  • App preferences and settings

This information is stored locally on your device.

Feedback you choose to submit

If you choose to submit feedback through the app, any information you include (such as written feedback or an optional email address) is provided voluntarily and used only to respond to your message or improve the app. Feedback is submitted using your device's email app.

Automatically collected information

GoalPath collects limited, anonymized app usage and diagnostic data to improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

This may include:

  • Feature usage and app interactions
  • Crash reports and error diagnostics
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version

This information does not personally identify you.

GoalPath does not collect:

  • Names
  • Required email addresses
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

2. Firebase Analytics & Crash Reporting

GoalPath uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to better understand how the app is used and to identify and fix crashes.

Firebase may process:

  • Anonymous usage metrics
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics

GoalPath:

  • Does not use Firebase for advertising
  • Does not track users across apps or websites
  • Does not link Firebase data to your identity

Firebase data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

  • Your personal content is stored locally on your device
  • If iCloud is enabled, your data may be backed up using Apple's iCloud services
  • GoalPath does not operate its own servers for storing personal user data

4. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.

GoalPath does not have access to:

  • Your payment information
  • Your Apple ID
  • Your billing details

5. Notifications

GoalPath uses local notifications to remind you about to-dos, tasks, phases, or goals.

  • Notifications are optional
  • You can enable or disable them at any time
  • Notification data never leaves your device

6. Third-Party Services

GoalPath uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store & StoreKit
  • iCloud
  • WidgetKit
  • Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics only)

GoalPath does not sell or share personal user data with third parties.

7. Data Deletion

  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device
  • Any data stored in iCloud can be managed or deleted through your Apple ID iCloud settings

8. Children's Privacy

GoalPath is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent changes.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

Email: goalpath@avfenterprises.com
